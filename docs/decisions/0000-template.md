## Context

What is the situation that forces a decision? State the problem, the constraints,
and the forces in tension (technical, operational, security, social). Write the
facts as they were *at the time* — an ADR is a historical record, not a live spec.
Keep it to what a future reader needs to understand *why*, not a tutorial.

## Decision

The change we are making, stated in active voice: "We will …". Be specific enough
that the decision is unambiguous, but no longer than it needs to be. If the
decision has hard rules (defaults, frozen constants, do-not-do boundaries), list
them here.

## Consequences

What becomes easier and what becomes harder as a result — both the good and the
bad, honestly. Note new obligations, follow-on work, and anything this decision
constrains or amends in another ADR.
