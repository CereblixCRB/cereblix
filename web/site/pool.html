Mining pool ● LIVE

Steady, low-variance rewards: the pool pays you proportionally to the work you submit, even when network difficulty is high. Point the stock miner at it - nothing else to install.

How to mine on the pool

Point any miner at a pool endpoint below - that's it, you're earning. Rewards arrive automatically as pool payouts once your balance crosses the minimum; the first payout takes ~100 blocks (~1.5-2h) while block rewards mature (100 confirmations). Your "share accepted" log lines are proofs cryptographically bound to your address - no one can claim your work.
