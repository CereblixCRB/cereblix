Wallet

Keys are created and stored only in your browser. The server never sees your private key - transactions are signed locally (ed25519).

⚠️ Save your private key somewhere safe. It is the only way to recover your wallet. Lose the key - lose the coins.

Unlock wallet

This wallet is password-protected. Enter your password to decrypt the key locally - it never leaves your browser.

Forgot password? Re-import your seed.

Protect with password
